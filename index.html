When and where is it?

It will be held on November 10th to the 11th inside Wentworth's Ira Allen building, located on Parker St, Boston, MA 02120.

What are the rules?

The maximum team size is 5 people. All projects must be started from scratch at the start of the hacking period and submitted to Devpost by the designated time. All participants must follow the MLH Code of Conduct, which will be enforced.

What should I bring?

You should bring your laptop, chargers, a valid ID, sleeping bag (optional) and pillow (optional).

Is there a schedule?

We are currently working on the schedule. Once we are done we will post it on this website and notify everyone by email.

Who can attend?

Any student who is 18+ years old can attend.

Is this event free?

Yes!

Do I need to be a computer scientist to participate?

Not at all! We aim to make this an inclusive event so that anyone can attend.

Do you offer travel reimbursement?

Unfortunately, we cannot offer travel reimbursement at this time.

What if there's a change in plans or an emergency?

In the event of a change of plans or an emergency, we will contact you via email.

Will there be free food and drinks?

Yes! We will have multiple meals scheduled throughout the event.

Is there a theme for the hackathon?

Nope! You are free to be as creative as you'd like.
